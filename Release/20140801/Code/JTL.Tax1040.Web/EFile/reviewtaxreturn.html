Review and Transmit

Return Submission

It's time to Submit your Tax returns. After successful submission of tax return, you can get confirmation from IRS in any of the 3 ways.

  1. In our TaxGarden website
  2. To your Email
  3. To Your Phone Number

Review all your tax Information and Filing Instructions, before transmit your tax returns.

Also, keep a print copy of tax return for your future reference.

If you want to change any of the details , please do change that by clicking the Concerned Menu links.

Note: Once you clicked "Transmit" button you can't cancel transmission.
