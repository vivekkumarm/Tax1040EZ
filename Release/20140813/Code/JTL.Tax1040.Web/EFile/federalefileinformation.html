Print Your Tax Returns: print

TaxGarden Federal and State returns can be directly filed to IRS or State authorities. These are the forms approved for filing. Simply print your return and mail it in. Please review your printed return(s) carefully before filing.

A copy of your return comes in handy if you need to amend, apply for a loan, or while preparing a return next year. It's also a very good idea to retain a copy of your tax returns for your records as a replacement copy is going to cost you much more.

Refer your Filing Instructions attached with your tax return:

The Filing Instruction report will provide you detail information on where to file. Kindly check your Federal and State Filing Instruction before sending a return copy mail to IRS and State.

If you want to file your State return along with Federal, then click "Continue with State Filing Info" button. To proceed with Federal return filing , then click "File Federal return" button.

To go back to your Filing Option Gateway page, click the ‘Start Over’ button.
