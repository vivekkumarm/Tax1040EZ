Review and Transmit

Return Submission

It's time to submit your tax returns. After Successful submission of tax return, you can get confirmation from IRS in any of these below 3 ways.

  1. In our TaxGarden website
  2. To your Email
  3. To Your Phone Number

Review all your Tax Information and Filing Instructions before transmitting your tax returns.

Also, keep a printed copy of the tax return for your future reference.

If you want to change any of the details, please do change that by clicking the concerned menu links

Note: Once you have clicked the "Transmit" button you can't cancel transmission.
